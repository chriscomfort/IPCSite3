BORN TO KILL

BORN TO KILL This movie is a coming of age, crime-drama that follows an orphaned Vietnamese-American teenage immigrant as he is initiated into the ruthless Vietnamese street gang during the late 80s in NYC's Chinatown. Not just your typical gangster movie, this film also touches on the struggles and achievements of Vietnamese-American immigrants, brotherhood, and finding one's place in the world. This is a film that strives to paint the many faces of Asian masculinity and the frenzied attempts to fit in a world they are barred from at every turn.
