ABOUT

 

Ivy Pham is a NY based Casting Director with 10 years of experience providing casting services for film, TV fashion shows, editorial, advertising, and street casting projects worldwide.

 

She is currently casting Born To Kill, a Vietnamese-American Indie Feature Film.

 

She has worked on projects in the past as a casting assistant at the NYC casting office of JV8INC under the acclaimed Jennifer Venditti (Uncut Gems, Euphoria, The Sympathizer). Her recent credits include HBO/A24's upcoming Vietnamese-American series The Sympathizer (directed by Park Chan-Wook, starring Robert Downey Jr., Nguyễn Cao Kỳ Duyên , Kieu Chinh) and Showtime/A24's upcoming The Curse (starring Emma Stone, Nathan Fielder, Benny Safdie).

 

Before making her way to Film & TV, she worked behind the scenes in the high fashion world for 8 years, working with world renowned fashion casting director Michelle Lee, casting runway shows, advertising campaigns, and editorials for clients such as Dior, Celine, Hugo Boss, Khaite, Tommy Hilfiger, Vera Wang, Shiseido, Zara, W Magazine, and Interview Magazine.
