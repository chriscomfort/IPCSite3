ABOUT

Ivy Pham is a NY based Casting Director with over 10 years of experience providing casting services for film, TV fashion shows, editorial, advertising, and street casting projects worldwide.

She currently works as a freelance casting associate at the NYC casting office of JV8INC under the acclaimed Jennifer Venditti. Her recent work includes A24's upcoming features MARTY SUPREME (Timothee Chalamet, Gwyneth Paltrow, Tyler The Creator), THE SMASHING MACHINE (Dwayne Johnson, Emily Blunt), Yorgo Lanthimos' BUGONIA (Emma Stone, Jesse Plemons), and HBO/A24's THE SYMPATHIZER (directed by Park Chan-Wook, starring Robert Downey Jr., Sandra Oh).

Before making her way to Film & TV, she casted in the high fashion world for 8 years, working with world-renowned CD Michelle Lee casting runway shows, fashion campaigns, and editorials for clients such as Dior, Celine, Hugo Boss, Khaite, Tommy Hilfiger, Vera Wang, Zara, W Magazine, and Interview Magazine.
